Introduction

The inspiration of this demo was about a well-known arcade game: Yugi-Oh! Duel Generation.

This app allows you to manage a mini-deck of Yugi-Oh Cards with the following particular rules:

  • Main deck contains at least 30 cards, and at most 50 cards. Side deck and extra deck each can contain 0 card or at most 15 cards.
  • Cards in this app are divided into 3 groups: Monsters (yellow), Spells (blue), Traps (red) and Specials (black).
  • At most 3 cards of the same name can be added to any deck. Extra deck can contain Special cards only.
  • Card Vault can contain any number of cards of any types. This is where to store the unused cards or card collection.

Feel free to play with this app.
